### Why Hydro90 Matters

In an era where water security and climate change are among the most pressing global challenges, **Hydro90** serves as a crucial platform for the next generation of water scientists. By fostering collaboration, sharing knowledge, and building professional networks, we're contributing to the development of innovative solutions for water-related challenges worldwide.

**Hydro90 welcomes all interested participants** who are passionate about hydrology, water resources, and environmental sustainability. Whether you're a graduate student, postdoc, or early-career researcher, there's a place for you in our growing community.

*For more information about joining Hydro90 or getting involved in our activities, please visit our website or reach out to our editorial team.*
